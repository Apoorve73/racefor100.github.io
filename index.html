Welcome to RACE FOR THE 100

  • After hiting PLAY button, you will be prompted to enter your name.
  • Then you have to guess a number in between 1 and 10, and type that in.
  • Then computer will similarly guess one such number, add it to yours, and return the SUM
  • The game keeps on going this way, alternatively.
  • The one who sum up to 100 at first place, will win the game.

Tip: If you get the trick, you will always WIN!
